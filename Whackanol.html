WHACK AN OL'

Trapped in a fever dream arcade, Eel Hamburger must fend off his tormenter, a king angel fish named Old Blue who is out for blood. A reimagining of real life events from the show Fishcenter Live.


PLAY IN BROWSER (Use Chrome)

ABOUT

Whack An Ol' was made for Adult Swim's website based on the events of the livestream show Fishcenter Live. The objective is to thwart attacking "Old Blues" with your eel bod. Use the WASD keys to move hamburger around and Space to Whack. Whack An Ol' was made using Unity 3D.
